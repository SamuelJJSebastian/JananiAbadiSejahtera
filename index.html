Company Profile

Established in 1998, PT. Janani Abadi Sejahtera has continuously grown into a full-fledged Inter-Island Feeder Operator and International Freight Forwarding Company that provides outstanding services to its customers.

We offer complete, integrated supply chain solutions tailored to your unique requirements. From specialized international shipping to domestic distribution utilizing our eco-friendly Euro 5 fleet and daily Jakarta-Surabaya rail transport, our dedicated team is committed to moving your business forward with maximum efficiency and reliability.
